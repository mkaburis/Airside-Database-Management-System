Manage Users

Add, delete, and update planes within the Airside DBMS. This utility may also be used to search for existing planes.

Manage Routes

Add, delete, and update routes within the Airside DBMS. This utility may also be used to search for existing routes. Routes can also be updated as active or not active.

Manage Flight Logs

Add, delete, and update flight logs within the Airside DBMS. This utility may also be used to search for existing flight logs.

Manage Passengers

Add, update, and delete passengers in the database. May search/update by attributes of passenger ID, first name, last name, data of birth, passport number, driver's license number, and home airport.

Passenger Flight Information Lookup

Search if a passenger is checked-in, connecting, and what flight(s) they are associated with.

Staff Flight Search

Query for particular flights by airline, concourse, route, etc.

Generate Flight Logs

View and genereate a download flight manifest for all passengers aboard a particular flight.
